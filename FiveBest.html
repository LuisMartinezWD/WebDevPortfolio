Ranking Current Wrestlers

WARNING! MY OPINION PRESENT!!

#1 Will Ospreay

Will Ospreay is one of the most
technical wrestlers alive today! His strong
style is incredible. He is unbelievably quick
and his work ethic is like no other.

#2 Jon Moxley

Jon Moxley, the Leader of the BlackPool
Combat Club a master of violence
in the ring. His style is so brutal and
gruesome that not many are willing to
recreate. He has had wonderful reigns as
champion and has stood as the pillar of
AEW since his debut in the company.

#3 Bryan Danielson

Bryan danielson is one of if not
the best technical and traditional wrestlers.
Very few still wrestle in this traditional
style.danielson has mastered this style and
blends it beautifully with a more modern
style of quickness and brutality.

#4 Seth Freakin Rollins

Seth Freakin Rollins is a mastermind
he has no issues controlling the crowd and
getting them to chant his name.
He has one of the most decorated records
with his championships and has stayed in
the main event scene for the past few years.
He's a revolutionary and deserves his spot.

#5 Roman Reigns

Roman reigns, AKA the Tribal Chief
has stood on top and dominated
the rest of the roster for three
years on end. He is the top guy. Roman
at this stage in his career has
had the highest of highs, but had to fight the
lowest of lows. Fans used to boo him, but
now they cant help but cheer.
