My Favorite Matches

WARNING! MY OPINION PRESENT!!

#1 Shield Triple
Threat

The WWE Shield Triple Threat match was
amazing because it wrapped up a long story
between Roman Reigns, Seth Rollins, and
Dean Ambrose. Their friendship and history
made it really intense. The way they
wrestled together,with Roman's strength,
Seth's flips, and Dean's brawling,
was really cool. The match was exciting and
kept us guessing, and you could tell
they were real friends,
making it a special moment in WWE.

#2 Kenny Omega vs Will
Ospreay

Kenny Omega vs Will Ospreay
was a spectacle of a match, it was
an incredible brawl the whole way through.
there was not a dull moment,
and this match is filled to the brim with
career defining highlights

#3 Bayley vs Sasha Banks

Bayley vs Sasha is a story of the
Woman on top who had it all, and
the woman fighting from the bottom for a
chance at it. Bayley was always an
underdog and sasha, her former tag team
partner, did everything in her power to keep
the title from bayley.But in this case,
Bayley came out on top

#5 Jon Moxley vs
